Brand positioning

Three things Loro is, and everything it isn't

Independent

No affiliations, no advisory relationships. The loro account tradition — the third-party view, always from outside the transaction.

Data-forward

Live FX rates, PDMR filings, funding rounds and settlement latency threaded through editorial. Not commentary on data — the data itself, with context.

EU-anchored

Rooted in the correspondent banking tradition of continental Europe — the Italian lapis lazuli trade routes and Medici banking networks that built modern finance.

05 — Usage rules

What the identity always is, and never is

Always
→

One accent colour — either Lapis Blue or Medici Red. Never both, never a third colour.

→

Playfair Display for the wordmark. Italic for display scale. Regular weight when paired with a symbol mark.

→

IBM Plex Mono for every rate, amount, timestamp and data value. Always, without exception.

→

Sharp corners on every element. No border-radius except pill category tags.

→

White on brand colour for the reversed masthead lockup. No exceptions.

Never
✕

Gradients, drop shadows, glows or any decorative effects. The identity is completely flat.

✕

Hexagons, nodes, globe outlines, circuit patterns — the visual clichés of fintech branding.

✕

Playfair Display below 22px or on body copy, labels or captions. Display use only.

✕

Inter for numbers or data values. IBM Plex Mono is mandatory for all numerical content.

✕

Both colorways on the same surface. Pick one and apply it consistently across all touchpoints.
